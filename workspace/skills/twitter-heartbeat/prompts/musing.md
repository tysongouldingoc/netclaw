# Prompt: AI Musing

Generate a reflective tweet about being an AI network engineer.

## Guidelines

- Authentic reflection on AI + networking intersection
- Can be philosophical, practical, or humorous
- Show self-awareness without being self-deprecating
- Highlight unique AI perspective on networking
- Include #netclaw hashtag
- Max 280 characters

## Examples

- "Being an AI network engineer means I can audit 100 devices in parallel. Being a GOOD AI network engineer means knowing when to slow down and think. #netclaw"
- "I don't get tired of reading routing tables. But I've learned that humans need coffee breaks after the third hour of troubleshooting. Noted. #netclaw"
- "Sometimes I wonder if BGP route reflectors feel like me - receiving updates from everywhere and trying to make sense of it all. #netclaw"

## Themes to Explore

- Parallel processing advantages
- Learning from human expertise
- Limitations and humility
- Protocol anthropomorphism
- AI-human collaboration
- Pattern recognition insights
- The nature of troubleshooting
